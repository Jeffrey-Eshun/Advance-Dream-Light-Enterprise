Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2006 by a team of printing engineers, Advance Dream Light Enterprise was built on a simple conviction: professionals who depend on large format machines deserve a partner who truly understands them. We started as a repair workshop and grew into the region's leading large format printing solutions provider.

Today we serve advertising agencies, architecture firms, engineering companies, sign shops, and in-plant print rooms — delivering hardware, expertise, and after-sales support that keeps operations running at peak performance.

Our team of factory-certified technicians holds specialist accreditations from HP, Epson, and Canon, and undergoes continuous training to stay current with the latest large format technologies.
